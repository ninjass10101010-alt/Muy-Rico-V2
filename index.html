Nuestra Historia
De Nuestro Horno a Tu Mesa

From Our Kitchen to Yours

Muy Rico was born from a simple hobby that grew into something much bigger. I'm Rebecca Garcia, the owner and head baker. Alongside my husband, Jeffery Garcia, and our five children — Bailey, Jasmine, Emily, Aurora, and Caspian — we pour our hearts into every recipe we create.

Our Family Goals

Here at Muy Rico, baking is a family thing. Our kids are our biggest taste-testers and toughest critics (and we wouldn't have it any other way!). Weekends are our favorite time of the week — that's when the whole family gathers in the kitchen to experiment with new recipes, tweak the classics, and find that perfect flavor that makes everyone say, “¡Muy Rico!”. Here in the Muy Rico kitchen we live by “Practice makes progress.” We aim to teach our children that you “simply do not fail. You fall and you get up and try again.”

With Latino Pride

Here at Muy Rico we are so proud of our Latino heritage. We love bringing a touch of Latin warmth and flavor to every bite — from soft golden breads to cookies and treats everyone can enjoy. We aim for every recipe to carry a piece of love and passion for who we are.

More Than a Bakery

Muy Rico isn't just about delicious bread and cookies — it's about bringing people together. We are a young business (less than a year old!), but we're already falling in love with this community that has welcomed us with open arms. Whether you stop by for a morning baked good or you are ordering to pick up for a special occasion, know that every bite is made with care, from our family, to yours.

Thank you for your patience as we continue to build our website. We look forward to preparing your orders!

From our kitchen to yours — ¡Buen provecho! — Rebecca, Jeffery, and the whole Muy Rico family!

¿QUÉ SIGNIFICA? ACTIVO

Cottage Food Law

Operamos de manera transparente bajo la Michigan Cottage Food Law. Preparamos alimentos no potencialmente peligrosos en nuestra cocina doméstica y los vendemos directamente a ti.

  • Panes, galletas, pasteles, tortillas y dulces hechos a mano.
  • Venta directa al consumidor sin cocina comercial.
  • Precios justos y productos frescos, hechos a mano.
PUBLIC ACT 51 DE 2025 LEGAL

Venta Online & Local

Según HB 4122, podemos vender por internet, entregar localmente o enviar directamente a consumidores dentro del estado de Michigan. No vendemos al por mayor.

  • Cada pedido va directamente de nuestra cocina a tu mesa.
  • Coordinamos punto de recogida seguro en Holland, MI.
