De Nuestro Horno a Tu Mesa

From Our Kitchen to Yours

Welcome to Muy Rico — where every loaf of bread and every cookie tells a story of family, tradition, and love for our community.

Muy Rico was born from a simple hobby that grew into something much bigger. I'm Rebecca Garcia, head baker and owner, and alongside my husband Jeffery Garcia and our five children — Jasmine, Bailey, Emily, Aurora, and Caspian — we pour our hearts into every recipe we create.

A Family Affair

At Muy Rico, baking is a family thing. Our kids are our biggest taste-testers and toughest critics (and we wouldn't have it any other way!). Weekends are our favorite time of the week — that's when the whole family gathers in the kitchen to experiment with new recipes, tweak the classics, and find that perfect flavor that makes everyone say, “¡Muy rico!”.

With Latino Pride

Proud of our Latino heritage, we love bringing a touch of Latin warmth and flavor to every bite. From soft, golden breads to cookies that taste just like homemade, every recipe carries a piece of who we are.

Fresh, Local & Always Made with Love

We believe the best ingredients come from right here in Michigan. That's why we source locally whenever we can — supporting the farmers and growers who make our community thrive. Every batch is made fresh, from scratch, in our kitchen.

More Than a Bakery

Muy Rico isn't just about bread and cookies — it's about bringing people together. We're a young business (less than a year old!) but we're already falling in love with this community that has welcomed us with open arms. Whether you stop by for a morning pastry or order cookies for a special occasion, know that every bite is made with care, by our family, for yours.

From our kitchen to yours — ¡Buen provecho! — Rebecca, Jeffery, and the whole Muy Rico family!

Michigan Cottage Food Law
¿QUÉ SIGNIFICA? ACTIVO

Cottage Food Law

Operamos de manera transparente bajo la Michigan Cottage Food Law. Preparamos alimentos no potencialmente peligrosos en nuestra cocina doméstica y los vendemos directamente a ti.

  • Panes, galletas, pasteles, tortillas y dulces hechos a mano.
  • Venta directa al consumidor sin cocina comercial.
  • Precios justos y productos frescos, hechos a mano.
PUBLIC ACT 51 DE 2025 LEGAL

Venta Online & Local

Según HB 4122, podemos vender por internet, entregar localmente o enviar directamente a consumidores dentro del estado. No vendemos al por mayor.

  • Cada pedido va directamente de nuestra cocina a tu mesa.
  • Coordinamos punto de recogida seguro en Holland, MI.
ETIQUETADO REQUERIDO OBLIGATORIO

Etiquetas & Alergenos

Cada producto lleva nombre, ingredientes por peso, alérgenos (trigo, leche, huevo, soya, cacahuate, nueces, sésamo) y peso neto.

  • Leyenda requerida: "Made in a home kitchen that has not been inspected by the Michigan Department of Agriculture and Rural Development."
Recogido en Holland Coordinamos punto de encuentro seguro en el área de Holland, MI.
